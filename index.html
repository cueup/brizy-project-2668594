Features

Skedu is a simple yet powerful automated booking specialist, working 24/7 to make sure your customers can book appointments or events and pay online even while you sleep!

Accept bookings anywhere

Get booking via the Skedu App, mobile responsive booking website or embed on an existing website.

Notifications via SMS/Email

Reminders to staff and clients whenever appointments are booked, cancelled or rescheduled. With push notifications on mobile for new booking information via the admin app.

Bullit Proof Security

256-bit end-to-end encryption secures you and your customers data from booking to confirmation. Hourly back-up keeps your data recoverable in case of disaster.

Accept Payments

Accept online payments & deposits through a range of payment processors like PayPal, Stripe or accept cash on onsite.

Integration & API

Facebook, Instagram, Google My Business native integration, Wordpress and other CMS systems or use our API to build your own custom integrations.

Customization

Adjust your booking website to fit your brand and customize all messages for notifications, reminders, feedback requests etc.
