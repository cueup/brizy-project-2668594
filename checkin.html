A better customer experience for contact tracing

A smart check-in solution that frees up staff from doing manual check-in of customers. No need to write down customer details with pen and paper, use SKEDU for secure, convenient check-ins.

For Business

Add your business to the app and a unique QR code will be generated. Display the QR code at the entrance on a mobile device or print and display on tables etc.

For Customer

Using the SKEDU App, customers can scan your QR code when entering the premises - with no need for contact. The customer details with a timestamp will be securely saved to your business profile.

How It Works

Add Your Business

Register your business and get your uniquely generated QR code.

Print or Display

Display the QR code on a mobile device or print for visitors to scan.

Trace and Report

Easily check and trace customer visits and stay compliant with government guidelines.

Customer Access

Visitors to your business downloads the app and scan your QR code on entry.

Customer Rewards

Incentivise visitors with tailored offers after they scan your QR code.
